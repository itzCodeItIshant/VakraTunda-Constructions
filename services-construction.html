The mandatory first aid requirements for a construction site are covered in The Health and Safety (First Aid) Regulations 1981. An appointed person must be on-site at all times; this is why you need to have multiple workers trained in first aid. Information on who the appointed persons on site and where to find them needs to be displayed.

An appointed first aid officer is a person who manages the first aid facilities and equipment on-site at an accident and emergency centre.
